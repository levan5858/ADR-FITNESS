Biography

Health is Wealth — the through-line of Andrei’s life.

Early foundations

Andrei grew up obsessed with aesthetics and athletics, training daily before dawn. His relentless curiosity led him to study programming, nutrition, and physique development through experimentation.

Those iterations became the backbone of ADR Fitness: measurable progress, refined form, and a lifestyle that balances intensity with longevity.

Modern mentorship

ADR Fitness now mentors creators, athletes, and executives across the globe, pairing data-driven protocols with design-led experiences. Every program is guided by Andrei’s mantra: execute with intent, recover with care, and keep the standard.
