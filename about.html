Biography

Health is Wealth — the through-line of Andrei’s life.

From Romania to the UK

Originally from Romania, Andrei moved to the United Kingdom with his family. At age 17, he began his fitness journey, transforming from facing weight issues and bullying to becoming a dedicated athlete. His early struggles became the foundation for his relentless pursuit of excellence.

Through disciplined training, nutritional precision, and unwavering consistency, Andrei developed the systems that would later define ADR Fitness—a gym brand co-founded with his business partner, primarily showcasing Andrei's proven methods and journey.

The ADR Fitness standard

ADR Fitness is a premium gym brand co-founded by Andrei Deiu and his business partner. While the brand represents a collaborative vision, it primarily highlights Andrei's IFBB Pro-level training methods, competition experience, and the "3D Gains" program he developed.

Every program is guided by Andrei's mantra: execute with intent, recover with care, and keep the standard. Health is Wealth—the through-line that connects every aspect of the ADR experience.
